Apple, Raisin, and Carrot Slaw

This is a description of the salad thing.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup yogurt (such as Icelandic Skyr®)
  • 1/3 cup light mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 5 cups chopped or shredded cabbage
  • 1/3 cup julienned carrot
  • 2 cups unpeeled diced red apple
  • 1/2 cup golden raisins
  • fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Whisk yogurt, mayonnaise, vinegar, poppy seeds, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Stir in cabbage, carrot, apple, and raisins. Garnish with fresh parsley.
